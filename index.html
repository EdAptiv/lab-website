Meet the team

The EdAptiv Lab unites a network of educational technology researchers to advance understanding of learning through data. Our work focuses on understanding the effectiveness of adaptive educational tools and leveraging their data to investigate the development of cognitive abilities, motivation, and learning behaviors. We draw on psychometrics, cognitive science, and motivation theory, using large-scale educational datasets and advanced statistical and computational methods. Our goal is to better understand how learners differ, how they change over time, and how data from real-world educational settings can inform theories of learning and development. A large part of our work also focuses on the development and evaluation of adaptive algorithms to track learners' ability, such as the Elo and Urnings algorithms.

We believe education should adapt to learners—not the other way around. Our research is supported by the Jacob's Foundation, NWO, and the European Union. Explore our publications here.
