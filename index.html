Meet the team

Based in the Psychological Methods Department at the University of Amsterdam, our lab unites a global network of educational technology researchers to improve learning through data. Drawing on psychometrics, cognitive science, and motivation theory, we design personalized, adaptive learning solutions.

We believe education should adapt to learners—not the other way around. Using AI and big data, we explore how people learn best and develop tools that support meaningful, individualized progress.
